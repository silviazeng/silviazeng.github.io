**P.S.** Once you start tracking who's stabbing whom in Florence, you also want to know who's invading whom in *Italy*. The peninsula in this period wasn't really one country — it was a shifting patchwork of duchies, republics, papal states and kingdoms, each with its own currency, army, and grudges.
